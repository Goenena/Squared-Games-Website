The Factory Must Fall

The Factory Must Fall is a factory-building PvP autobattler where your factory's produced units fight other players. Draft buildings from a random shop, design your factory, optimize resource allocation and army composition to climb the ladder.

Wishlist Now on Steam

About the Game

The Factory Must Fall combines base-building, automation, autobattler, and RTS genres to create a unique competitive PvP game!

Draft buildings from a random shop, design your factory and optimize resource allocation. Once you are ready, launch your design into battle with another player!

Features

Build your Factory! Grow your Factory!

  • Setup production lines to create auto-attacking units
  • Draft buildings from a shop and grow your factory
  • Start the battle when you are ready
  • Lean back and watch the battle unfold

Explore Build Possibilities!

  • Experiment with endless build possibilities!
  • Want to rush your opponent early and win with a cheesy build?
  • Want to turtle up and win lategame using superior tech and strong units?

Defeat your Enemies! Climb the Ladder!

  • Challenge your friends directly in Custom PvP matches
  • Face the builds of real human opponents on the ranked ladder
  • Gain rank as you defeat more and more skilled opponents
